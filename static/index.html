How to use

  1. Enter a name and press Dispense Account.
  2. An account will automatically be created for you, but it will take a sec. While you're waiting, copy the email and open the Taco Bell app on your phone.
  3. Once the "Get Login Code" button on this website isn't gray, paste the email into the Taco Bell app and press Log In.
  4. Return to this website, copy the verification code, and paste & log into the Taco Bell app.
